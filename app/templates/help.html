Valid names

Only lowercase alphanumeric characters, dots, and dashes are allowed. Names must start with a letter and cannot end with a dash. The characters, which are delimited by dots, have to form a valid name. Dots cannot be consecutive or be at start or end of the name.

Network Issues

Caution

Caution is displayed when delegate participation rate drops below 80%.

Severe network problems

Severe network problems is displayed when delegate participation rate drops below 60%. Low delegate participation makes blockchain forks likely.
